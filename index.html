About Me

Hello! My name is Wahyu Akhmad Fadillah, and I was born on August 12, 2005, in Tegal. I am currently a third-semester student pursuing a D4 degree in Computer Engineering at Politeknik Harapan Bersama Tegal.

I have a deep passion for design, writing, and coding. Through this portfolio, I aim to showcase my creative works and technical skills. Design is my way of expressing ideas and crafting engaging user experiences, while writing allows me to share my thoughts and stories in a more profound manner.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I am an avid sports enthusiast. Engaging in physical activities not only helps me maintain my health but also fuels my energy and motivation in daily life.

I believe that the synergy between creativity and technology is key to developing innovative solutions. Through this portfolio, I hope to demonstrate my abilities and collaborate with like-minded individuals who share a similar vision.
